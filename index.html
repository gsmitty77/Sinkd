Sinkd Rules

House Rules We Play With

Scoring

1Table Hit
3Sink (bounce-ins count)
2Tink
2FG Off
2FG Def
1FIFA

Self Sink is an automatic loss. Bounce-ins count.

Throw Requirements

  • The die has to be at least 10 feet over the table.
  • The die has to hit the line or the opponent's side.
  • Body catches are awarded the point.

FIFAs & Field Goals

  • FIFAs are playable off the side of the table.
  • No knees allowed for FIFAs.
  • Defensive field goals have to be blocked before they fall off the table.
  • Defensive FIFAs do not have to hit the table, but they are allowed to.
  • Call FIFA and hit the table: house penalty.
  • You can call FIFA as many times as you want, but scoring is capped at three successful FIFA points per turn: three standard FIFAs, two field goal FIFAs, two standard plus one field goal FIFA, or one field goal plus one standard FIFA.

Throwbacks & Table Stays

  • If your toss is short and you catch it on the back of your hand, you are awarded your throw back.
  • If the die hits the opponent's side and stays on, the thrower can guess the die number without looking. If correct, the thrower gets their toss back.
  • If the die hits the opponent's side, stays on, and lands on 5, group penalty.

Responsible Use

Sinkd is intended for responsible, legal-age players. Follow your local laws, know your limits, and use house penalties responsibly.

About Sinkd

Built For The Group Chat

Why Sinkd Exists

Sinkd was built by a college student who wanted game nights to feel less messy and a lot more official.

It started with a simple problem: friends were tracking scores in the Notes app, arguing over who had the better record, and forgetting the details that made each match worth talking about later.

Sinkd keeps the stats, records, leagues, tournaments, and bragging rights in one place, so the group can settle debates with real numbers and get back to playing.

The Vibe

Built for friendly competition, day time matchups, house rules, and the kind of stat lines that keep a group chat alive.

Have fun, keep it responsible, and let the numbers do the talking.

Sinkd Privacy

Privacy Policy

Overview

Sinkd is a dice game stat tracking app for regular games, tournaments, leagues, friends, achievements, rankings, and notifications. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By using Sinkd, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as your email address and login provider.
  • Profile information you choose to add, including nickname, preferred partner, cup color, and notes.
  • Game and stat information, including scores, wins, losses, sinks, tinks, table hits, field goals, FIFAs, self sinks, achievements, rankings, and game history.
  • League information, including league name, description, logo colors, membership, roles, invites, roster data, rankings, league chat messages, and league game logs.
  • Friend information, including friend requests, accepted friends, invite emails, and display names.
  • Notification information, including league invites, friend requests, achievement unlocks, personal updates, read status, and timestamps.
  • Device/browser information that may be collected automatically by hosting, authentication, database, or analytics infrastructure, such as IP address, browser type, device type, and basic logs.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage accounts.
  • To save profiles, stats, game history, achievements, rankings, friends, leagues, and notifications.
  • To sync league data across users and phones.
  • To let league owners, co-leaders, refs, and members use the correct league permissions.
  • To send or show in-app notifications for achievements, friend requests, league invites, and updates about you.
  • To maintain, debug, secure, and improve the app.

Local Data vs Cloud Data

Some app data is stored locally on your device, such as local player lists, regular games, big games, tournament data, and local profile stats. Local data may not sync across devices.

League data is stored in a shared cloud database so league members can see synced rosters, league games, rankings, stats, invites, and league chat updates.

Sharing With Other Users

Information you add to a league may be visible to other league members, including your nickname, cup color, role, league stats, achievements, rankings, and league game history. Friend requests and league invites may show your nickname, display name, or email to the other person.

Service Providers

Sinkd uses third-party services to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication/database services, Netlify for hosting, and Google sign-in if you use Google authentication. These providers may process information according to their own privacy practices.

Notifications

Sinkd may show in-app notifications for friend requests, league invites, achievements, and updates about you. Browser or phone notification popups require device/browser permission. Notification records are automatically cleared after a short period so your inbox stays current.

Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile information in My Profile.
  • You can delete local players from Settings unless the name is tied to My Profile.
  • You can leave leagues if you are not the owner.
  • League owners and co-leaders may manage league members and league data.
  • You can decline notification popups when the app asks for permission.
  • You can request that account or cloud data be removed by contacting the app owner.

Data Security

Sinkd uses account authentication and database access rules to help protect cloud data. No system is perfect, so do not add sensitive personal information to profiles, notes, league descriptions, or game logs.

Children

Sinkd is not intended for children. If users apply house penalties or legal-age activities while playing, they are responsible for following local laws and using the app responsibly.

Responsible Use

Sinkd does not require or encourage unsafe behavior. Use the app as a stat tracker, follow local laws, and make responsible choices during gameplay.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Sinkd changes. Updates will be posted in the app. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the Sinkd app owner.
